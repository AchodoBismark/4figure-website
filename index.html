About us

Who we are

We are a team of technocrats, harnessing technology, to foster social interactions and cross-cultural relations across Africa. Here at 4 FIGURE, we value human capital and the potentials locked up in cultural reserves.

The rich heritages and learning, that can be unlocked with the proper application of easy-to-use technology in the 21st century where everything has gone digital. We are currently building the first cross-cultural communications and learning platform in Africa.

Our Vision is to create and sustain a digital world, truly united by creativity and technology.
Our Mission is to facilitate more value-added and appreciative communication, leveraging on technology, with an aim to grow a truly united world.
Our Goal is to deploy an app annually. Be at the forefront of tech integration in culture and education in Nigeria and Africa by 2025. Deeply showcase Nigeria to the world in its true colours.
Our Objectives is to harness the tech potential of African youth, by setting up tech hubs that allow for growth and expression without prejudice. Enable easier cross-cultural communication, learning and appreciation.
Our Brand Values are Curiosity, Creativity, Innovation, Commensurate Value and Excellence. .
